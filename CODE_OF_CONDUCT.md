# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Forum to be a welcoming, harassment-free space for everyone, regardless
of experience, background, or identity. Participation is expected to be
respectful and constructive.

## Our standard

Forum is a tool for structured debate, and we hold ourselves to the same bar we
ask of the models:

- **Argue on the merits.** Critique ideas, not people.
- **Treat every contributor as a peer.** No favoritism, no gatekeeping by status.
- **Disagree well.** Strong, evidence-based disagreement is welcome; personal
  attacks, harassment, and bad faith are not.

## Scope and enforcement

This applies to all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions) and
public spaces when representing the project. To report unacceptable behavior,
open a confidential issue or contact the maintainer directly. Maintainers may
remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code, and may ban
repeat or severe offenders.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
